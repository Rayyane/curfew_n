Iconic Bar

CURFEW is a one of a kind bar nestled just below Fort Worth’s iconic Sundance Square, adjacent to the Historic "Hell's Half Acre".

Cowtown meets Funkytown

Mixing unique cocktails, local artists, interactive decorum and charm to curate an experience that stays true to it's Fort Worth roots. CURFEW is the embodiment of Cowtown meets Funkytown
